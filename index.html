How to survive in the wilderness

The three lures to bring while hiking alone

10/##/2025 By Dmytro Maidan

Only need 3 lures

Why should you use them?

To survive you need food, what if you don’t know how to hunt and you're afraid of animals? Fishing will be your next choice for the source of protein, and not to only eat berries. While the berries could give you a little energy boost, you cannot survive while eating only berries, you need protein, and other elements that come from fish, it will fill you for a while and also give most of the elements to survive.

Worms is a great choice for catching variety of fish, but you may not find them, or even just simply don’t want to use them you could use a paddle tail/flukes (kinds of soft plastic swimbaits), if you want to stand out for feeding bass, use white colored, or use bright colored lures in murky waters and use natural colored soft plastics in clear waters. What does it have a ton of weeds? Use something that has its hook covered, like Texas jigged soft plastic or a football jig, and a frog that can go though the thickest stuff that you throw it in since it’s a topwater and it got its hooks fully covered
